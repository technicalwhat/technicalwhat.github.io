Cat Planets

Part trailer, part game installation, Cat Planets was presented at FMX 2018. It builds up on the quite hard mission it is to understand or control (ha!) cats.
Watch the trailer: https://vimeo.com/317243347

Responsible for:
Game development and co-direction, laser tracking, laser pointers design and construction.

Core team:
Trailer director: Yi "Yinfinity" Luo; Trailer co-director: Michael Bohnenstingl; Game co-director: Patrik Knittel; Producers: Michael Bohnenstingl & Lukas Ritter

Done at Filmakademie BW, Germany.
