Flamenco

A short film that attempts to test a different way to approach post-production for stop-motion.
For integrating the digital asset on the set, footage was shot of a moving light, and that material was used to create an equivalent light response (i.e. relighting).
For an in-depth explanation of the process, refer to the publication “Freezing Fire – Automated Light-Passes for Stop-Motion VFX”, presented at SIGGRAPH ’21.
https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3450623.3464640

Responsible for:
Direction, animation, lighting and capture automation, robot programming and relighting R&D.

Lead team:
Producers: Laura Messner, Josephine Ross; Compositor: Denis Krez.

Done at Filmakademie BW, Germany.
