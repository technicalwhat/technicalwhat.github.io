Love and 50 Megatons

An explosive love story that required a vast amount of VFX, Love And Fifty Megaton was an invitation to VFX supervisor Denis Krez to take a risk and leap into the world of in-camera VFX with real-time engines before it was such a common technique.
For more information, check the making-of video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1aMOyJATUyM
For a more in-depth conversation about this work, check the talk The Power of Real-Time Collaboration as presented at PICTURE THIS_20.

Responsible for:
Preparation of digital environment, camera tracking and re-projection, on-set tools development.

Core team:
VFX supervisor: Denis Krez; VFX producer: Josephine Ross; Director: Cornelius Schick.

Done at Filmakademie BW, Germany.
